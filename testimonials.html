What our customers say?

The extensive menu has the Vietnamese basics like spring rolls, eggrolls , banh mi ( baguette sandwiches) , noodles, rice dishes and stir-fries. Here are just a few dishes that customers like a lot! Spaghetti Basil…



I am a huge fan of Vietnamese cuisine and really wanted to find a good Vietnamese restaurant and Banh Mi place near my work. I checked here on Google and find out Vietnam Kitchen. I went to try it for the first time and was instantly a fan.

The restaurant is small and more of a take out place. However, there is nothing small about the courtesy of the employees and taste of the food. I grabbed a order of Spaghetti Basil and a Spam and BBQ pork Banh Mi. They were both amazing but the Spaghetti Basil was really the show stopper. The flavors of the Spaghetti Basil and portion size was great for the price.

Definitely a great find!!

Make sure to try the Spaghetti Basil!

Steve, Louisville, KY



I was introduced to Vietnam Kitchen by a friend who catered her birthday party with them. Food was very fresh and flavorful. Since then, I have gone back for take out and have always been really happy about their service, friendliness and quality of food. The staff is always helpful and super friendly.

Ordering over the phone is really convenient and it’s ready to pick up in less than 15 minutes for a dinner order. Their prices can be a little higher than other Vietnamese restaurants, but quality is better and the customer service is awesome. Also they are a family restaurant who also own Pho Ba Co down the street, so I love to support local places when I can!

Delicious food!!

Dara, Louisville, KY
